Uncovering Tourism Behaviour in Malaysia

Exploring tourist arrivals, state trends, gender patterns & flight connectivity (2020-2024).
Geographic Overview
Malaysia attracts many international tourists. Known for its cultural diversity, modern infrastructure, and tropical landscape, Malaysia offers a unique blend of Malay, Chinese, and Indian influences. Tourism is a vital economic sector, contributing significantly to GDP and employment. This section provides geographic information showing where tourists originate and where do they enter from.

Trend of Tourist for States in Malaysia
Each states in Malaysia has distinct attractions and infrastructure that results in different volumes of visitors. This section shares the patterns of tourists that came into Malaysia from different states.

Which states attracts the most tourists in Malaysia?

Johor emerges as the dominant tourism state, largely driven by strong inflows from neighbouring country Singapore which was Malaysia's top tourism contribution.

How Malaysia's top tourism states recover?

Johor and Selangor (KL) shows a strong recovery from COVID-19 pandemic in tourism, with a large increase in number of tourists.
